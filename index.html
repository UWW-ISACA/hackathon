What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a collaborative event where individuals gather to work on software or hardware projects within a limited timeframe. Participants form teams, brainstorm ideas, and develop their projects from scratch. The atmosphere is intense and fast-paced, with participants fueled by passion and creativity. At the end of the hackathon, teams present their projects, competing for prizes or recognition.
